What can you do?

Show off

Spent the last week building that Dragon's Keep? Stashed away a treasure in the highest room of the tallest tower? Why not show it off!

Movies

Create them videos, movies or memes with style. Cough up the dough to pay the actors. Remember, though, a good shot takes time to set up and get right.

Benchmarking

One of the nice things with reproducible movement is that you can use it for benchmarking. Included here too, just an option away.

More Features

You can play music, automatically follow things, automatically focus on moving objects, fly along a path, walk slowly. And much much more. Imagination is really the only thing stopping you.
